Connecting an RC Controller via USB

Connect your RC controller to your laptop using a USB cable. Most modern controllers will be recognized automatically as a joystick input. Check under “Game Controllers” settings to confirm input detection before use in simulators or software.

Wiring Orqa FPV Goggles to Laptop

Use an HDMI cable from the laptop to the goggles’ HDMI input for video output. If powering via USB, connect through a 5V to 9V converter cable. Ensure your power source can supply at least 2A. Avoid using both barrel and USB power simultaneously.
